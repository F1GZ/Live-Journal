Post 1: My greatest coding investment so far

I wanted to share the reasons why my raspbery pi workspace is my best investment so far for my coding journey

1. Seperate space
Having a seperate space is extremly good and helpful for keeping me productive as I now have a dedicated space compared to using my gaming pc with all it's distractions
2. Simplicity
As of right now I only have my monitor, keyboard, mouse, water, notebook, pens and plant pot on my desk. This greatly helps as it all isn't very stimulating and the desk itself is very small which stops me from puting anything else on it that may distract me
3. The computer's specs
Having a low speced computer is benificial as it stops me from playing too many games on it but is able to run all my code and web courses.
